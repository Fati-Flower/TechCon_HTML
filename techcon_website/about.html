Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a local meetup for tech enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most influential global technology conferences. Over the years, it has become a hub for innovation, thought leadership, and community collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire and connect innovators from around the world by creating a platform for sharing ideas, exploring future technologies, and building inclusive tech communities.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Ada Techwise – Renowned AI researcher who delivered a keynote on ethical AI in 2022.

  • Linus Smartdev – Open source advocate and creator of several popular developer tools.

  • Noura Datahub – Data scientist and educator, known for her work on inclusive data practices.
